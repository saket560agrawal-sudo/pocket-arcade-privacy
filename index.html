POCKET ARCADE

Privacy Policy
Effective date: 1 July 2025

1. Overview

Pocket Arcade (the "app") is a casual arcade game published by the Pocket Arcade team. This policy explains what information the app handles, how advertising works inside the app, and the choices you have.

In plain English: we do not ask you to sign up, we do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information. Play is fully anonymous.

2. Information we collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app works without any account.

Scores, coin balance, settings, and play time are stored locally on your device using the operating system's secure storage. They never leave the device, are never uploaded to any server we control, and are removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

Pocket Arcade is free to play. To cover development costs, the app shows ads provided by Google's AdMob advertising network, including:

  • Banner ads at the bottom of most screens
  • Interstitial full-screen ads between games
  • Optional rewarded-video ads that grant an in-game revive (shown only after you tap the "Watch Ad" button)

AdMob is operated by Google LLC. To serve contextual or personalised ads, Google and its third-party vendors may collect an anonymous advertising identifier (the Android Advertising ID or Apple's IDFA), basic device information (device model, OS version, IP address, coarse language/region), and general interaction data with the ad (impression, click, video completion).

This information is collected directly by Google — not by us — and is governed by Google's own Advertising Privacy Policy.

You can reset or limit the advertising identifier at any time from your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → "Reset advertising ID" / "Delete advertising ID"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → "Personalised Ads" (off)

On first launch the app may show the Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) consent screen where you can choose whether Google may use your data for personalised ads.

4. Children

The app is rated for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has been shown inappropriate ads, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

5. Your choices

  • Opt out of personalised ads from the UMP consent screen or via your device's ad-ID settings above.
  • Uninstall the app to delete all local data (scores, coins, settings).

6. Data we do NOT collect

  • Name, email, phone, or other contact details
  • Physical or precise location
  • Photos, contacts, microphone, or other on-device media
  • Payment details (handled exclusively by Apple or Google)

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top of this page will always reflect the latest revision. Material changes will also be announced inside the app's Settings screen.

8. Contact

Questions or requests about this policy? Email us at pocketarcade.support@gmail.com.
